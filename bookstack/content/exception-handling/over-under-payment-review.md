# Over and Under Payment Review

## Purpose

Explain how to handle payments processed above or below the expected amount.

## Business Meaning

An over or under payment happens when the payment reference matches, but the receipt amount differs from the expected payment batch amount.

In reporting this appears as amount variance review.

## Examples

- customer paid more than the expected invoice amount
- customer paid less than the expected invoice amount
- exchange, fee, or correction changed the processed amount
- partial payment was received against a full expected amount

## Reconciliation Treatment

An amount variance is not a clean allocation match.

The reference may be reliable, but the amount variance must be reviewed before allocation.

## Procedure

1. Confirm the matching reference.
2. Compare expected amount against receipt amount.
3. Calculate the variance.
4. Check whether the variance is explained by a known fee, refund, or correction.
5. If explained, document the treatment.
6. If not explained, escalate to the finance operations owner.
