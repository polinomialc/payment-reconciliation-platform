# KPI Definitions

## Open Batch Amount

Total value of payment batches that are not fully allocatable.

## Review Items

Count of items requiring operational review, including evidence review, amount variance review, and rejected card transactions.

## Chargebacks

Count of receipt exceptions classified as chargebacks.

Chargebacks are reported separately from normal allocation evidence because they represent disputes, reversals, or payment adjustments.

## Matched Amount

Total amount of payment batches with receipt evidence that supports allocation.

## Aging Bucket

Age band used to prioritize open balances.

Common buckets:

- 0-7 days
- 8-30 days
- 31-60 days
- 60+ days
