# Escalation Matrix

## Purpose

Define when open items should move from analyst review to operational escalation.

## Escalation Triggers

- high-value open balance
- item older than the allowed aging threshold
- repeated missing payment-channel mapping
- rejected transaction without later successful payment
- unexplained over/under payment
- chargeback affecting already allocated payment

## Ownership

Operations owns first review.

Finance owns accounting treatment and final escalation decisions.

Data owners support rule or mapping corrections.
