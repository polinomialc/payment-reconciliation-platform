# Vendor Independence

One of the main strategic goals of this project was to reduce operational dependence on third-party reconciliation platforms.

## Previous Constraints
- rule changes depended on vendor processes
- troubleshooting was slow and bureaucratic
- platform behavior was not fully owned by the internal team
- licensing and vendor costs added long-term friction

## Internal Platform Benefits
- direct ownership of reconciliation logic
- rapid rule adjustments after internal validation
- lower operational dependency
- easier troubleshooting
- better control over data structures, outputs, and governance

This is a technical improvement, but also an operating-model improvement.
